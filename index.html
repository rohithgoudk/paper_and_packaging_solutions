Who we are

From pulp to package, we run the whole sheet.

Paper & Press started in a single die-cutting shop and grew into a full paper-goods manufacturer without ever losing the parts our first customers liked: fast samples, honest pricing, and a crew that answers the phone.

Today we design structure, print the outside, and box up what's inside — for e-commerce brands shipping their first hundred units and manufacturers shipping their millionth.

How a project moves

Four stages. Same team the whole way.

01 / Design

Structure & art

Our structural designers model your box to the product, then our art team lays out the print file around it.

02 / Prototype

Sample & sign-off

A physical white or print-accurate sample ships to you within 48 hours for approval before tooling.

03 / Produce

Short or long run

From 250-unit digital runs to 500,000-unit flexo orders, produced across our six facilities.

04 / Deliver

Palletized & tracked

Freight booked, palletized, and tracked to your warehouse or 3PL — with reorder points set for next time.
