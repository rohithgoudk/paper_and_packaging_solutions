Who we are

From pulp to package, we run the whole sheet.

Pulp & Press started in a single die-cutting shop and grew into a full paper-goods manufacturer without ever losing the parts our first customers liked: fast samples, honest pricing, and a crew that answers the phone.

Today we design structure, print the outside, and box up what's inside — for e-commerce brands shipping their first hundred units and manufacturers shipping their millionth.

What we make

Six product lines, one production run away.

01

Corrugated shipping boxes

Single, double, and triple-wall stock built to your SKU dimensions, tested for the drop and the belt.

02

Kraft mailers & bags

Peel-and-seal mailers and twist-handle bags in FSC-certified kraft, printed edge to edge.

03

Custom retail boxes

Rigid and folding cartons with foil, emboss, or soft-touch finish — designed to be unboxed, not just opened.

04

Protective inserts

Die-cut honeycomb and molded pulp inserts that hold fragile product still without foam.

05

Eco void-fill

Crinkle paper, honeycomb wrap, and starch-based air pillows — every filler here breaks down.

06

Custom print & finishing

Flexo, litho, and digital short-run printing, plus foil stamping, spot UV, and die-cutting in house.

How a project moves

Four stages. Same team the whole way.

01 / Design

Structure & art

Our structural designers model your box to the product, then our art team lays out the print file around it.

02 / Prototype

Sample & sign-off

A physical white or print-accurate sample ships to you within 48 hours for approval before tooling.

03 / Produce

Short or long run

From 250-unit digital runs to 500,000-unit flexo orders, produced across our six facilities.

04 / Deliver

Palletized & tracked

Freight booked, palletized, and tracked to your warehouse or 3PL — with reorder points set for next time.
